# HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing employee data to uncover key insights related to **attrition, performance, and workforce trends**.  
The goal is to help HR teams make **data-driven decisions** instead of relying on assumptions.

The interactive dashboard provides a clear view of employee behavior, retention patterns, and critical HR KPIs.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Understand the main factors contributing to employee attrition  
- Identify high-risk departments and roles  
- Analyze the impact of salary, experience, and performance on retention  
- Support strategic HR decision-making using data  

---

## 📊 Key Insights
- Attrition trends across departments and job roles  
- Relationship between salary levels and employee turnover  
- Impact of years at company on retention  
- Performance rating vs attrition analysis  
- Gender and age-wise workforce distribution  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies Used
- **Data Analysis:** Python (Pandas, NumPy)  
- **Visualization:** Power BI 
- **Data Cleaning & EDA**  
- **Dashboard Design & Storytelling**  

---

## 📈 Dashboard Features
- Interactive filters for department, job role, and experience  
- KPI cards for attrition rate, total employees, average salary  
- Visual charts for quick and clear insights  
- User-friendly layout focused on business understanding  

---

## 🧠 Business Impact
This dashboard can help HR teams:
- Reduce employee confirmation
- Improve retention strategies  
- Identify problem areas early  
- Make informed hiring and workforce planning decisions
